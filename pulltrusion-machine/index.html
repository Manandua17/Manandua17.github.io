Overview

The Pulltrusion Machine is an open-source filament extruder that transforms recycled PET bottles into 3D printing filament. Based on the PETamentor design, it uses a heated nozzle, puller motor, and temperature controller to produce consistent, high-quality filament.

Components

  • Frame & Cutter: Custom chassis slicing PET bottles into uniform strips.
  • Heater & Nozzle: Interchangeable tips for different filament diameters.
  • Puller Motor: DC motor drawing molten PET steadily.
  • Temperature Control: W1209 module for precise heat regulation.
  • Power Supply: 12–24V adapter.

Process

  • Cut PET bottles into thin ribbons.
  • Feed ribbons into heated nozzle.
  • Pull molten PET into filament.
  • Spool the filament for use.
